Puma Punku is the Place for You!

If you enjoy hiking and exploring the world, Puma Punku is the perfect place for you. It is part of a large temple composite that is part of Tiwanaku located in western Bolivia. This ancient site it believed to date back to AD 536 and is known as one of the most intriguing ancient sites on the planet. Some people believe Puma Punku is evidence that aliens visited the Earth.
